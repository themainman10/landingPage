Pesce Deluxe

esce Deluxe is a buffet catering to all who are interested in the pescatarian lifestyle, or similar. Our buffet will be opening soon Winter 2022 in the Southeast MI area. Sign up now for more updates by email!

If you are health conscious or just looking to become a “clean eater” at Pesce Deluxe you are guaranteed to shed a few pounds. We also have vegan friendly menu options because our goal is to strive for a healthy community one bite at a time.

Here at Pesce Deluxe you can get your main source of animal protein from fish and other seafoods. We exclude meat and poultry! We carry wild-caught foods which are considered to be healthier and leaner. You will also find a variety of plant-based, dairy, and egg foods to your avail. Our sources are traceable, sustainable, and responsible. We are committed to providing healthy choices to the public.
